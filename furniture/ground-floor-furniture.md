# Ground Floor Furniture Proposal

Status: Proposed, revised after user correction.

## Living Area

Room facts used: living north wall is 16'11"; TV wall is on the west side with about 11'6" usable TV-unit length. Living is entered from the foyer at the northwest side. A 5" sunken living floor is planned after a 1ft offset from walls.

### Recommended Layout

Use an L-shaped seating layout facing the west TV wall.

| Item | Size | Material / finish | Placement |
|---|---:|---|---|
| L-shaped sofa, long side | 10ft to 11ft x 36in to 38in deep | Oatmeal / mushroom / warm grey performance fabric | Along north wall, facing west TV wall. |
| L-shaped sofa, return side | 7ft to 8ft x 36in to 38in deep | Same fabric | Along east side, returning toward open south side. |
| Lounge chairs | 2 x 28in to 32in wide | Teak/cane frame or upholstered accent chairs | Toward south/open side, angled inward. |
| Coffee table | 48in x 28in, or 42in round | Stone top, wood, or wood + metal | Centred on rug. |
| Rug | 9ft x 12ft | Low-pile wool / performance rug | Under seating zone. |
| Side table | 18in to 22in | Wood / stone / black metal | Beside sofa or between chairs. |
| Floor lamp | 5ft to 6ft high | Brass/black with warm shade | Near lounge chair. |

### Sunken Living Floor

- Keep a 1ft perimeter offset from walls before the 5" drop.
- Use the same tile on step tread and riser.
- Add a subtle brass/black edge profile or low-level LED under the step edge for safety.
- Confirm sofa dimensions only after marking the sunken area on site.

## Living TV Unit

| Part | Recommended size | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Total designed wall zone | About 11'6" wide | On the west wall. |
| Central feature panel | 7ft6in to 8ft6in wide | Extend vertically to beam/top feature height. |
| Floating console | 10ft to 11ft long x 16in to 18in deep x 16in to 18in high | Drawers below, ventilated AV cavity. |
| TV | 75in ideal, 65in acceptable | TV centre around 42in to 45in from finished floor. |
| Side vertical panels | 18in to 24in wide | Fluted teak/walnut, one or both sides. |

### TV Unit Notes

- Keep the console floating to make the room feel lighter.
- Ply and laminate are acceptable: use 18mm BWP/BWR plywood carcass with matte/suede/anti-fingerprint laminate shutters.
- For the double-height feature, combine laminate with microcement, stone-look slab, veneer, or fluted panels so it does not look flat.
- Avoid high-gloss laminate and heavy full-brown panelling.
- Add hidden cable conduit from console to TV if wall chasing is still possible.
- Provide ventilation holes for electronics.

## Decor

- One tall plant near the corner window or north-window edge.
- One medium plant near the TV console.
- One large artwork only if there is a clean non-TV wall for it.
- Cushions in off-white, taupe, muted sage, and one rust/brass accent.
