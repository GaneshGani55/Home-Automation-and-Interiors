## Where to Spend vs. Save

| Spend here | Save here |
|---|---|
| Tiles — large format, good brand (Kajaria/Somany) — they last 20+ years | Vanity cabinet — local carpenter with good marine ply, save vs. branded modular |
| Waterproofing — never cut corners on this | WC brand — Parryware/Hindware is reliable; Kohler/TOTO is prestige, not necessity |
| Brushed gold fittings — stick to one brand for consistency (Jaquar or Kohler) | Rain shower head — local brands perform the same at 1/3 price |
| Toughened glass partition — don't go below 8mm | Niche LED strip — any reliable IP65 strip works |

---

## Execution Sequence (for contractor coordination)

1. **Civil rough-in** — plumbing concealed pipework, electrical conduit, concealed cistern box
2. **Waterproofing** — floor + lower walls (allow 48hr cure)
3. **Niche boxing** — build recessed niche boxes in south wall before tiling
4. **Tiling** — floor first, then walls. Niche interior last.
5. **Glass partition** — after tiling, glazier installs
6. **Fittings** — plumber installs WC, faucet, shower, drain
7. **Electrical** — fixtures, PIR switch, exhaust fan
8. **Vanity & accessories** — mirror, cabinet, seat, hooks, towel bar
9. **Grouting & sealing** — final grout + silicone bead at all joints
